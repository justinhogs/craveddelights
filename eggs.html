CHEESY SCRAMBLED EGGS

For these, you'll need:

4 eggs
3 teaspoons of butter
2 slices of cheese

STEP 1: BEATING THE EGGS

For this, there's nothing much. However, ensure you are still able to see some egg white as to not overbeat it.

STEP 2: MELTING THE BUTTER

Put roughly 2 teaspoons of buttern into the pan until it begins to sizzle.

STEP 3: LOWER THE HEAT

Butter burns extremely easily, and we don't want that. We also want the eggs to cook slowly.

STEP 4: POURING THE EGGS IN

Pour it in!

STEP 5: FOLD IN

This is probably not the way you're used to cooking them, but here, we are going to push in the sides of the eggs towards the center very gently repeatedly until the eggs have solified slightly.

STEP 6: POUR IN SINFULNESS

This is when you throw in the final teaspoon of butter and the cheese to give the eggs a creamy texture.

STEP 7: FINISH UP

Try your best to fold the egg on top of itself repeatedly. This allows the top of the eggs to cook as well. Sadly, there's no proper way to do this and it'd probably look like a mess.

STEP 8: TAKE THEM OFF

Take them off while they are still slightly runny. The residual heat will cotninue cooking them.

AND.... DONE!

That's it! Soft, creamy and buttery eggs.
